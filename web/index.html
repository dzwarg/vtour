Oh, The Places You Will Go

Welcome

Use this map to find photographs that have been geotagged on flickr *.

To start, search for the place you are interested in. The search box is in the upper right hand corner. Go ahead and find your place now. I'll wait.

By the way, if at any point, you want to start again from scratch, click on the "Start Over" button at the top.

Now that you've found a place to investigate, start clicking on the map. Each click will place a marker, and search flickr for photographs near the place you clicked.

As you are clicking around, photos taken near the markers will appear in the light strip below the map. To view a larger version of an image, click on it in the light strip.

Below the strip of images, the number of total photos found near this point will be displayed. Also, this will show you which set of images in the search result you are viewing.

To move forward or back in your "tour", click on the right or left side of the light box, respectively.

* Support for panoramio is coming soon.
